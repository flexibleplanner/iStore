How to Delete Your Data in Idea Store App

If you wish to delete your data, you can do so by following the steps below:

Automated Method

Click the link below to send an automated data deletion request:

Send Data Deletion Request

Manual Method

Follow these steps to delete your data manually:

  • Step 1: Log in to your email.
  • Step 2: Create a new mail with recepient "**Insert email**".
  • Step 3: Set email title to "Data deletion request".

Via the application

Follow these steps to delete your data via the application (if you have it installed, or you can re-install it):

  • Step 1: Log in to your account.
  • Step 2: Go to your profile tab. And click 'Edit profile'
  • Step 3: Click the 'Delete Account' button. You may be asked to relogin, as the operation is sensitive and may require fresh login.
