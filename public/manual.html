What is Idea Store

Whether you are an ambitious person that wants to improve its life. Or you have big ideas and dreams to make significant advances for humanity. Or simply looking to build up a small business to gain your daily life. Or if you are searching for clever ideas of investments... The Idea Store is built for you.


The Idea Store:
  • Provides you with a personal workspace to manage and evolve you ideas.
  • Allows you to share your ideas on the Store with other users. Either to help other people to improve their life and find solutions. Or to share dreams that you wish will be realized and benefit other people.
  • Gives you access to a common free store of Ideas. Where you can find solutions for creating small businesses, starting new projects, investing money... And much more.
  • Provides you regular and customized suggestions and ideas in your areas and centers of interests.

The ideas can belong to various areas (trade, real estate, technology...etc), and various categories (small businesses, big projects, financing sources, investments...etc).
